Calibration

While calibrating, have face pointing towards webcam. Do not over express*** blinks. After calibration, keep head the same distance from webcam.

Typing

Selecting Letters

1. Blink To Select A Row

2. Blink Again To Select A Letter

Whatever area is shaded is the current selection

Webcam

Fitting Tracking To Face

Make sure your face in centered in the green box and your head is level with your computer. If you're having trouble, try moving slowly towards, then away from your webcam.

Keep Face Inside Green Tracking Box

Tracking is only active within the webcam green box overlay

Remain Still

Eye will Work best if you stay still after completing calibration
