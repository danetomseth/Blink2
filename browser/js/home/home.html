What is Blink?

Blink is a low-cost assisted communication web-app for people suffering from locked-in syndrome

The project was inspired by a Ted Talk and developed as a part of a capstone at Fullstack Academy

This is the beta website that is simply a proof of concept. The fully functional webapp can be navigated without over touching the mouse or keyboard.
