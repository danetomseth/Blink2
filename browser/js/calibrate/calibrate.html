Calibration

What is Calibration?

To accurately detect your blinks, the app will calculate the average area of your eye when open and closed to register a blink. This "blink profile" is unique to each user. An example blink profile is displayed on the right.

Calibrate Open Eye

The first step will measure the average area of your open eye.

Calibrate Blink

Measure the eye area during a blink to compare to open eye, establishing the threshold of blink detection

Check calibration

View the live tracking data of your personalized eye measurements and test-drive the app

How To Blink

EYEsee is sensitive enough to detect sublte blinks.

Blink normally while calibrating and typing. There is no need to over-express the blink action.
